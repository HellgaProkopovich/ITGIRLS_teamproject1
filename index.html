Coffee of the day

Americano coffee (Italian: Caffe Americano) is an espresso diluted with hot water. The presence of foam (cream) depends on how coffee and water are mixed. Attention! The concepts of "American" and "American coffee" should not be confused. n the second case, this refers not to diluted espresso, but to a drink prepared through a filter system.
